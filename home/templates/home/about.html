Our Story

Background

We began with a simple observation: many commercially available bath bombs were packed with harsh chemicals, artificial ingredients, and tested on animals. We knew there had to be a better way to experience the joy of a bath bomb without compromising your health or values. This realization fueled our passion to create bath bombs that were not only effective but also ethically sourced and handcrafted with love.

Vision

We see a world where self-care and sustainability go hand-in-hand. Our mission is to craft luxurious bath bombs with simple, vegan-friendly ingredients that are never tested on animals. We use recyclable packaging and source ingredients responsibly, because bathtime shouldn't cost the Earth.
