How can we help you?

We hope you have found an answer to your question. If you need any help, please contact us via email.

Great news! Most of your orders will be delivered to you within 2-3 working days.

However, there are a few things to keep in mind:

  • For some special items, it may take up to 5 working days if they need to be sent from our distribution center first.
  • Deliveries are made Monday to Friday, 9am to 6pm.
  • Weekends (Saturday and Sunday) are not considered working days and won't be included in the delivery timeframe.

Think your order is late?

No worries! If your order should have arrived by now and you haven't received it, just send an email to info@bombasticbaths.com and we'll be happy to look into it for you.
